About Us

Viswam Catering Services (Taste That Talks) is a professional and premium catering services company established in 2008. For nearly 17 years, we have been providing comprehensive services to meet all the culinary needs of our customers in Nellore. Our goal is to deliver a unique dining experience with every delicious meal we serve. Our motto, "Delivering smiles with every celebration," reflects our commitment to providing the utmost satisfaction to our clients at affordable prices. We are dedicated to using uncompromisingly high-quality ingredients, generous portions, exceptional hygiene standards, freshly prepared spices, transportation, meticulous presentation, and paying attention to every detail of dishes.
